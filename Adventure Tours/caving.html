Caving in Sagada

Caving in Sagada offers an exhilarating adventure as you explore the renowned Sumaguing Cave, famous for its breathtaking rock formations and impressive stalactites and stalagmites. The cave's intricate passages provide a unique underground experience. Sagada is also known for its intriguing hanging coffins, a traditional burial practice that reflects the area's rich cultural history. In addition to caving, you can enjoy stunning scenic viewpoints, such as Echo Valley and the picturesque rice terraces, making Sagada a perfect destination for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts alike.
