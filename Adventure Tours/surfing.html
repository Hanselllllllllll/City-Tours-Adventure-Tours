Surfing in La Union

La Union offers an amazing surfing experience with consistent waves suitable for both beginners and advanced surfers, featuring popular spots like San Juan, Urbiztondo Beach, and Balaoi Beach, along with various surf schools for lessons and board rentals. The vibrant surf culture includes beach bars and competitions, making it a fun place to unwind after a day on the water, with the best surfing conditions typically from October to March.
