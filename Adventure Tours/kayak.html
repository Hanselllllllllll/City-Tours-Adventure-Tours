Kayaking in El Nido

Kayaking in El Nido is an ultimate adventure! You can explore stunning lagoons like the Big Lagoon and Small Lagoon, where crystal-clear waters meet towering limestone cliffs. Guided tours often include stops at hidden beaches for swimming and snorkeling, making it a perfect way to delve into the area's natural beauty.
