Scuba Diving in Puerto Galera

Puerto Galera, a popular diving spot in the Philippines, offers a variety of underwater experiences. With its clear waters, colorful reefs, and diverse marine life, it's suitable for both beginners and experienced divers. Some popular dive sites include White Beach, Aninuan Island, and Verde Island Passage. Remember to choose a reputable dive center, book in advance, respect the marine environment, and consider a dive package for a more convenient experience.
