Intramuros

Intramuros is a historic walled city in Manila, Philippines. Its a UNESCO World Heritage Site with Spanish colonial architecture. You can find Fort Santiago, Manila Cathedral, San Agustin Church, and Real Fuerza de Santiago there. Enjoy guided tours, horse-drawn carriage rides, shopping, and delicious Filipino food. Additionally, Intramuros is a great place to learn about Philippine history and culture. You can also take a leisurely stroll through the cobblestone streets and admire the beautiful architecture.
