locos Sur Museum

The Ilocos Sur Museum is a cultural institution in Vigan City, Philippines. It showcases the region’s history and heritage. The museum has artifacts from prehistoric times, Spanish colonial era, and local crafts. You can learn about the Ilocanos’ way of life and traditions. The museum also hosts temporary exhibitions and events. It’s a great place to learn about the Ilocos region’s history and culture.
