Dinosaur Island

Dinosaur Island, located in Clark, Pampanga, is a unique theme park where visitors can experience life-sized dinosaur models. The park features engaging educational shows that provide insights into dinosaur history. Guests can interact with animatronic dinosaurs that move and make sounds, offering thrilling encounters. There are also interactive areas where children can participate in activities like fossil digging. The park includes food stalls and souvenir shops for snacks and dinosaur-themed merchandise. It’s a family-friendly destination that’s easy to access from Manila and perfect for a fun day out.
