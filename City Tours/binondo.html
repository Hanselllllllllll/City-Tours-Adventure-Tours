Binondo China Town

Binondo, known as the oldest Chinatown in the world, is a vibrant district in Manila, Philippines. Rich in history and culture, it serves as the center of Chinese commerce and community in the country. Binondo is famous for its bustling streets filled with shops, restaurants, and food stalls offering an array of authentic Chinese cuisine, from dumplings to savory noodles. The area is also home to historic temples, such as the Binondo Church, which reflect its deep cultural roots. With its lively atmosphere and diverse culinary offerings, Binondo is a must-visit destination for those seeking to experience the unique blend of Filipino and Chinese cultures.
